# Downloaded Papers

This directory contains research papers relevant to the investigation of induction heads and their role in leakage/repetition.

1.  **In-context Learning and Induction Heads** (Olsson et al., 2022)
    -   `2209.11895v1_In-context_Learning_and_Induction_Heads.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Seminal paper defining induction heads and their link to ICL.

2.  **Induction Head Toxicity Mechanistically Explains Repetition Curse in Large Language Models** (Shuxun Wang et al., 2025)
    -   `Induction_Head_Toxicity_v1.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Directly addresses how induction head "toxicity" causes repetitive generation (leakage).

3.  **The Evolution of Statistical Induction Heads: In-Context Learning Markov Chains** (Edelman et al., 2024)
    -   `2402.11004v1_The_Evolution_of_Statistical_Induction_Heads_In-Co.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Studies how transformers learn in-context statistics, which might be a source of leakage.

4.  **Next-token pretraining implies in-context learning** (Riechers et al., 2025)
    -   `2505.18373v2_Next-token_pretraining_implies_in-context_learning.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Links pretraining data properties to the emergence of induction heads and ICL.

5.  **Language Models "Grok" to Copy** (Lv et al., 2024)
    -   `2409.09281v2_Language_Models_"Grok"_to_Copy.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Connects the copying mechanism (induction heads) to training dynamics.

6.  **Mechanistic Data Attribution: Tracing the Training Origins of Interpretable LLM Units** (Chen et al., 2026)
    -   `2601.21996v1_Mechanistic_Data_Attribution_Tracing_the_Training_.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Shows how repetitive structural data in training catalyzes induction heads.

7.  **Overthinking the Truth: Understanding how Language Models Process False Demonstrations** (Halawi et al., 2023)
    -   `2307.09476_Overthinking_the_Truth.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Identifies "false induction heads" that attend to and copy false information from previous demonstrations.

8.  **Understanding and Controlling Repetition Neurons and Induction Heads in In-Context Learning** (2025)
    -   `2507.07810_Repetition_Neurons_Induction_Heads.pdf`
    -   *Why relevant*: Directly addresses controlling repetition driven by induction heads.
